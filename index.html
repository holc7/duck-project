    Ever talked to a rubber duck about your code? It's not quackers! It's called 'rubber duck debugging', where developers chat with their ducky pals to untangle tricky code. Who knew ducks were such good listeners? Maybe next time, they'll bill us for their time! And while they're great for a laugh and a float in the tub, their true genius lies in helping us find those elusive coding mistakes. Just remember, if the duck starts talking back, it might be time for a coffee break!

    ABOUT THIS PAGE: This page serves as a guide for new developers to understand the importance of problem-solving techniques. By understanding the principles of Rubber Duck Debugging, developers can enhance their debugging skills and resolve issues more efficiently.
